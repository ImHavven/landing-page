Pizza is not a 'trend' it's a way of life

About 350 slices of pizza are consumed every second in the US. You can't have a list of interesting facts about pizza without mentioning just how much Americans love pizza. Americans consume an average of 350 slices of pizza every second. That is equivalent to about 100 acres of pizza a day and 21,000 slices a minute.

Some Facts
Over 5 billion pizzas are sold worldwide each year
October has been celebrated as National Pizza month since 1987.
94% of Americans eat pizza regularly.
Thin crust pizza is the most popular form of crust worldwide.
